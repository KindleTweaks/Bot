Jailbroken Kindles are a great space for making applications, games & utilities.

Here are some common resources:

- [Kindle Hacking](https://kindlemodding.org/kindle-hacking/index.html) - Learn about Jailbreaking internals
- [Mesquite Guide](https://github.com/penguins184/Penguins-Kindle-Wiki/) - Learn about Exploits, and making simple beginner-friendly HTML apps.
- [GTK Guide](https://kindlemodding.org/kindle-dev/gtk-tutorial/index.html) - Make GTK apps in C or C++.
- [Go-GTK](https://github.com/clintharrison/template-kindle-gtk/tree/clint/go-gtk) - Make GTK apps in Golang.

Have fun!
