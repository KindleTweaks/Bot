In order to install KOReader, you can use 2 methods.

Recommended Method: Using KindleForge

1. Open KindleForge
2. Locate the KOReader details card
3. Press `Install Package`.
4. Wait, KOReader will install shortly!

After this, you can open KOReader through the automatically-applied scriptlet or through the KUAL/PEKI menu.

Alternative Method: Traditional Install

1. Download KOReader from [here](https://github.com/koreader/koreader/releases).
    - If your Kindle is on `5.16.3`+, download the `kindlehf` package.
    - Otherwise, download `kindlepw2` (`5.16.2.1.1` and below)
2. Unzip the release on your computer.
3. Plug in your Kindle, copy the `extensions` and `koreader` folders to Kindle root.
4. If prompted, confirm replace/merge existing files.
5. Unplug your Kindle, KOReader is installed!

Afterwards, navigate to the KUAL/PEKI menu and launch KOReader that way.
